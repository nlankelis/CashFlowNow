# CashFlowNow

**Instant Cash for Your Invoices**  
A web-based invoice factoring platform that lets SMEs and freelancers sell unpaid invoices (individually or in bulk) for immediate cash.

![CashFlowNow Marketing Site](https://nlankelis.github.io/CashFlowNow/CompleteLogo.png)

[![Visit Marketing Site](https://img.shields.io/badge/Visit_Marketing_Site-blue?style=for-the-badge)](https://nlankelis.github.io/CashFlowNow/)

## The Problem
Small businesses and freelancers often wait 30–90 days to get paid. This creates severe cash-flow problems, delays growth, and can even threaten survival.

## The Solution
CashFlowNow is a modern invoice factoring platform where users can:
- Upload single or multiple invoices
- Get instant cash offers (powered by AI-powered OCR for fast processing)
- Sell invoices individually or in bulk
- Track payment history and completed deals

## Key Features
- **Smart Invoice Upload** – Drag & drop with automatic OCR extraction
- **Instant Offers** – Real-time cash advance calculations
- **Bulk Factoring** – Sell multiple invoices at once
- **Dashboard** – History of uploaded and completed invoices
- **Secure & Simple** – Clean, user-friendly interface for non-technical users

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend**: TypeScript, React (Vite)
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI/Flask)
- **AI/OCR**: Optical Character Recognition for automated invoice reading
- **Deployment**: Marketing site hosted on GitHub Pages
